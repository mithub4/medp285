Meet our friends!

Winston

Winston is a blue-phase white tree frog who was rather laid back, introverted, and looooved filling his tummy with big, tasty bugs! He loves sitting in his water bowl and climbing on top of his brother.

Robin

Robin, just like his brother, is also a blue-phase white tree frog with a more outgoing personality, loving to climb up to high places, and hop around! He has a particular afinity for fingers.. so watch out!!

Jax

Jax was a dog that was rehomed due to being lonely and now lives in a house with lots of constant attention! He slobbers a lot and sulks when DD goes on car rides without him.

DD

DD came from the street into a loving home and is very loved. DD and Jax have a mother-son relationship, she has taught Jax everything he knows about being the best dog ever!

Pancake

Pancake is an even tempered, but rather food motivated American shorthair! She enjoys cuddling and relaxing with her owner at the end of a long day

Aaliyah

Aaliyah is a chihuahua-pomeranian mix that is an expert at protecting her humans- when she's not taking a nap! She's an expert at finding the best sunspots, and loves cuddles!
