Meet Aaliyah

Aaliyah

Chihuaha Mix, 4 y/o, Female

Aaliyah is a Chihuaha mix, but is often mistaken for a fox! She loves to sleep, and can be found sunbathing by the window when she's not guarding her owners from dangerous intruders (squirrels and the occasional neighbor)! She's very sweet, and loves curling up on the couch to watch TV with her owner at the end of the day!

Aaliyah's favorite treats:

  • Chicken
  • Peppers
  • Crackers
