Meet Winston

Winston

White tree frog, 4 y/o, Male

Winston is an introverted, laid back white's tree frog - except when it comes to food! He enjoys fooling around with his brother, Robin, occasionally- climbing all over his tank walls and making a splash in his water bowl! He's also, unfortunately for his owner, very curious and an avid explorer, always attempting to escape during feedings! He does need his space though - you'll often find him nestled atop his hammock or on top of his coconut hide when he needs to rest.

Winston's favorite treats:

  • Mealworms
  • Dubia roaches
